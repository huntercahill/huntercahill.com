About

Hunter Cahill is an Austin, Texas singer-songwriter. His debut collection Some Things You Don’t Know About Me arrived between 2021 and 2022 — six self-recorded singles, music that was honest enough to keep its face hidden. The press noticed. Austin Town Hall drew comparisons to David Bazan; Soundsphere called it “stunning.” He released all of it wearing a wolf mask.

The Estuary Sessions, recorded with engineer Matt Gerhard, go further back than the coping does — to a religious upbringing where love and fear were indistinguishable, and what that does to a person.
