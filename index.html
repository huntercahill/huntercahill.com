About

Confessional music that kept its distance. He’s back without the mask.

Hunter Cahill’s debut EP Some Things You Don’t Know About Me arrived between 2021 and 2022 — six self-recorded singles about keeping it together in public and falling apart in private. The press noticed. Austin Town Hall drew comparisons to David Bazan; Soundsphere called it “stunning.” He released all of it wearing a wolf mask.

After some quiet, Cahill returns without one. New recordings made at Estuary Recording with engineer Matt Gerhard go further back than the coping does — to a religious upbringing where love and fear were the same, and the questions it left him asking. The songs trace what follows: learning to live with it, and the loss that sets in when you can’t.
