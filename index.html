About

The ache and honesty of Hunter Cahill. The sound of life, in its most raw state of uncertainty and doubt — comforting in its profound vulnerability.

Austin, Texas singer-songwriter Hunter Cahill found solace in making music: an escape from the year that shook the world, when failing mental health rattled him. With precision and the soulful integrity of sound, these songs were delivered one by one until they formed a whole — songs for guitar, and songs for piano.

The music reaches out with piercing authenticity and heart. A voice committed to the cause, pulled from the depths to detail the pain and complexities of sadness, anger, and betrayal. The process proved an essential form of therapy and recovery — and in turn, the songs offer the same assisting hands to anyone who delves in and listens.

It is the meeting between the story told and the reality experienced. We blur the lines, fill in the gaps, and present only our best selves — even when we’re feeling our worst. Don’t hide in your darkness. We suffer most when we do it in silence.
