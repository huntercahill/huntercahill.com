Listen: Hunter Cahill — “Never Again”

August 6, 2021

Soundsphere Magazine highlighted Hunter Cahill’s track “Never Again” as a standout alternative rock offering. The reviewer praised the composition, noting it features “beautiful guitar lines meet country beats and a unique, empathetic vocal style that will motivate and inspire.”

The publication drew comparisons to established artists, stating: “Fans of Manchester Orchestra and REM, rejoice. This is stunning stuff.”
