Hunter Cahill — Laughable

February 7, 2022

The review explores how Hunter Cahill’s song “Laughable” functions as emotional catharsis. The Texan artist created the track while experiencing mental health struggles, channeling feelings of sadness, anger, and regret into the work.

Cahill’s vulnerability is a central strength of the single, as he openly addresses self-doubt and insecurity in ways listeners may recognize from their own experiences. The musical backdrop draws comparisons to bands like Arcade Fire and folk musicians such as David Bazan and Frank Turner, offering what the reviewer describes as “a comforting lair” for the emotional content.

The piece emphasizes that “music is medicine” and that Cahill’s openness about his struggles demonstrates admirable artistic honesty. The review concludes by noting the artist is now in a healthier state, partly due to using songwriting as a healing mechanism.

Originally published by Panda Collective — February 2022.
