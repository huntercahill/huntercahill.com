Hunter Cahill Shares “Falling Down”

October 1, 2021

The piece discusses Hunter Cahill’s track “Falling Down” from his album Some Things You Don’t Know About Me. According to the review, the song features “a bouncing indie rock vibe” built on rhythmic foundations with piano and guitar elements.

The reviewer highlights the song’s thematic depth, noting that it addresses serious subject matter about coping mechanisms. The critic appreciated both the musical composition and emotional relatability, suggesting the song achieves an intimate connection with listeners through its authentic subject matter while maintaining musical vitality.

The article notes that Hunter Cahill released monthly tracks from his album throughout 2021, with additional releases promised before year’s end.

Originally published by Austin Town Hall — October 2021.
