Hunter Cahill Shares “Goddamn”

July 1, 2021

The review highlights Hunter Cahill’s track as a compelling indie rock ballad. The piece notes that “you’ll get a muted guitar that opens up into the perfect rock ballad; it kicks in with this perfect punch of classic indie rock.”

The critic draws vocal comparisons to David Bazan and praises the chorus particularly, describing how “the guitars hang up in the air, before crashing down with this emotional punch that eventually evolves into this soaring melodic burst.”

The review concluded by noting that listeners should anticipate monthly single releases from Cahill leading up to his new LP announcement.

Originally published by Austin Town Hall — July 2021.
